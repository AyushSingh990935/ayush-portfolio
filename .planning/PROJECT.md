# Ayush Singh Portfolio

## What This Is

A premium, highly interactive personal portfolio website for Ayush Singh. It serves to showcase his unique background bridging chemical engineering, operational strategy at Nayara Energy, and his current MBA journey at IIT Kharagpur, aimed at impressing recruiters and peers with a modern, high-tech aesthetic.

## Core Value

To present Ayush's diverse professional journey and leadership experience through a visually stunning, premium digital experience that leaves a lasting impression.
